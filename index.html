Welcome to Planet Earth 🌍

Our home. Our responsibility. Let's explore and protect it together.

🌋 Formation of Earth

Earth was formed about 4.5 billion years ago from a massive cloud of dust and gas. Through intense heat and collisions, a molten planet emerged, eventually cooling to form a crust — giving rise to oceans, land, and an atmosphere.

🦕 Age of Dinosaurs

For millions of years, dinosaurs ruled Earth. This prehistoric era was full of lush jungles, volcanic eruptions, and massive reptiles. It ended with a catastrophic asteroid impact, changing Earth's history forever.

👨‍🌾 Human Evolution

From early cave dwellers to modern civilizations, humans have adapted to Earth's changing environment. With innovation and discovery, we've explored continents, created cultures, and shaped the world in countless ways.

🌪️ Modern Challenges

Today, Earth faces serious threats — climate change, pollution, deforestation, and species extinction. These challenges demand urgent action from all of us to protect our planet’s future.

🌱 How You Can Help

Every small action counts. Reduce plastic, conserve water, plant trees, support clean energy, and educate others. Together, we can build a greener, healthier Earth.
